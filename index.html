Welcome to New Vision Pentecostal Ministry

New Vision Pentecostal Ministry is a prophetic prayer-based church focused on spiritual growth, discipleship, and community transformation. We host weekly services, youth programs, outreach activities, and a range of ministries that support families and young people. Through worship, teaching, and prophetic ministry we seek to uplift lives and bring hope to our community. Join us for inspiring worship, dynamic preaching, and hands-on outreach.

New Vision Pentecostal Ministry

New Vision Pentecostal Ministry — Building lives through prayer, prophetic ministry and community action. We run programs for children, youth and adults and partner with local agencies for outreach. Our services combine worship, sound teaching, and practical care for families facing difficulty.

Vision

Our vision is to raise disciples and impact the world through Christ-centered living.

Mission

Our mission is to spread the Word of God and build a strong, prayerful community.
